Roscoe's Chicken Adobo

Look no further for the best testing Chicken Adobo recipe, because we have it here!


Follow along our take of the Filipino Chicken Adobo, and make sure you prepare extra servings of steamed rice! (Second helpings guaranteed.)

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 whole chicken, cut into parts
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 onion, chunked
  • 5 leaf bay leaves
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 1 cup white vinegar
  • 2 cups water
  • 8 black peppercorns
  • steamed rice for serving

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Heat vegetable oil in a large pot over medium-high heat. Cook chicken pieces until golden brown, 2 to 3 minutes per side.

  2. Step 2

    Add garlic, onion, bay leaves, brown sugar, soy sauce, vinegar, water, and peppercorns. Stir and bring to a nice simmer.

  3. Step 3

    Cover with lid and simmer over medium heat for 15 minutes. Remove the lid and simmer for another 15 minutes.

  4. Step 4

    Serve with rice.
